To see cookies in dev console: 
- Click the '>>' 
- Click 'Application'
- On the left side, under Storage, click 'Cookies', and then the webpage you're on 

Session vs. Cookies:
- Session stores cookies in the form of an array, hides values, requires a secret key, need to pop values to delete them
- Cookies store individual bits of data separately
